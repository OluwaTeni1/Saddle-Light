    *WHO WE ARE

    Saddle Light Charity Foundation is a nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering young people and helping those in need. Since its founding in 2021, the Organization has provided a variety of services to people.

    One of the Organization’s primary goals is to empower young people to reach their full potential. Through mentoring programs, after-school activities, and job training, the charity helps youth develop the skills and confidence they need to succeed. The charity also offers food assistance programs to those in need, helping to ensure that no one goes hungry.

    Also the Organization’s most successful programs is its empowerment program. This program matches at-risk youth with caring and responsible adults who can help them navigate the challenges of adolescence. The program provide support, guidance, and encouragement, helping the youth to stay on track and make positive choices. The Organization also offers school programs that provide a safe and supportive environment for young people to learn and grow. These programs offer tutoring, arts and crafts, sports, and other enrichment activities.
