    WHAT WE DO

    "Education is not just about providing children with the skills they need to succeed in life, it is also about creating a more just and equitable society. When children have access to quality education, they are better equipped to make informed decisions, to get good jobs, and to contribute to their communities in meaningful ways. In addition, education can help break the cycle of poverty and improve health and well-being.

    In addition to helping individuals, education can have a positive impact on entire communities and societies. For example, by providing education to girls, we can help reduce the rates of child marriage and teenage pregnancy.

    Furthermore, studies have shown that even a single year of additional schooling can increase a person's earning potential by 10-20%. This means that education can help lift families and communities out of poverty, and can even lead to economic growth on a national level.
